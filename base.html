Surgical Classes

These surgical classes will add padding or margins to elements.

p Apply padding to the element.
m Apply margin to the element.
a Apply padding or margins to all sides.
v Apply padding or margins to top and bottom of the element.
h Apply padding or margins to the right and left sides of the element.
t, r, b, l Apply padding or margins to top, right, bottom, or left sides respectively.
s, m, l Choose either small, medium, or large sizes for the padding and margins.
